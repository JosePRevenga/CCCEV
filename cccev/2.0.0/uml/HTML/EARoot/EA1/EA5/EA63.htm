A distinct intellectual creation (i.e., the intellectual content). For example, the abstract concept of the legal resource; e.g. "act 3 of 2005" (adapted from Akoma Ntoso)<br/><br/>Additional Information:<br/>1) The ELI Legal Resource is a subclass of the IFLA LRM Work class (https://www.ifla.org/publications/node/11412).<br/><br/>2) A legal resource can represent a legal act or any component of a legal act, like an article. Legal resources can be linked together using properties defined in the ELI model (see https://publications.europa.eu/en/web/eu-vocabularies/eli).<br/><br/>
